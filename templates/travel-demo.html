The Photographer

Chasing light across the world for fifteen years.

I'm Noah West — a travel and landscape photographer based between Austin, Texas and wherever the next photograph lives. I've shot on six continents, from the Icelandic highlands to the Atacama Desert, always searching for the moment when light transforms an ordinary place into something unforgettable.

My work has been published in National Geographic Traveler, Condé Nast, Outside Magazine, and licensed by tourism boards across four countries. I also offer limited edition fine art prints for collectors and interior designers.

About the Photographer

Hi, I'm Sarah — and I believe every family has a story worth telling.

I've been photographing families in the Austin area for over 8 years. What started as a passion project has grown into a full-time studio dedicated to capturing genuine, joyful moments — not posed, stiff portraits.

Every session I do is relaxed, fun, and designed around your family. I want your kids to be themselves, your partner to stop worrying about the camera, and for everyone to walk away with photos they actually love.

Based in Austin. Available for outdoor sessions, in-home sessions, hospital newborns, and on-location shoots across Central Texas.

The Process

From Idea to Live Website

No guessing. No ghosting. Every project follows a clear 5-step process — you always know what's happening and what's next.

01

Discovery Call

We start with a free conversation. I want to understand your business, your customers, what you've tried before, and exactly what you need your website to do. No generic templates. Everything starts with your specific situation.

  • 15 to 30 minute call via phone, Zoom, or text
  • We talk about your goals, timeline, and budget
  • You get honest advice on what will actually work
  • Zero commitment required
02

Design and Planning

Before a single line of code is written, we lock in the design direction. You'll see the color palette, layout plan, and overall look and feel. We make sure you love it before moving forward.

  • Custom design tailored to your brand and industry
  • Review the sitemap with every page planned before build
  • You approve the design direction before we start coding
  • Revisions included at this stage
03

We Build It

This is where the magic happens. You sit back while I build your fully custom site. Every section is coded specifically for your business. I send progress updates so you always know where things stand.

  • 100% custom code. No drag-and-drop page builders.
  • Mobile-first and SEO-ready from day one
  • Progress check-ins throughout the build
  • Fast turnaround. Most sites done in 2 to 4 weeks.
04

You Review and We Refine

When the site is ready, I walk you through every page. You give feedback, request changes, and we fine-tune until it's exactly right. Revisions are part of the deal, not an add-on charge.

  • Full walkthrough of the finished site together
  • Revision rounds included in every package
  • Tested on mobile, tablet, and desktop
  • You sign off before anything goes live
05

Launch and Ongoing Support

We go live. Your domain is connected, your site is live, and you start showing up where your customers are looking. And if you ever need updates, changes, or new features, CRJ has you covered.

  • Domain and hosting setup handled
  • Google Analytics and Search Console connected
  • Post-launch support included
  • Monthly maintenance plans available for continued updates

Common Questions

Pricing FAQ

Do I pay all at once?

For one-time builds, we typically do 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. Monthly support is billed at the start of each month.

What if I need something bigger?

The Premium package starts at $800 and scales with complexity. Large e-commerce stores or custom web apps will be quoted individually after our discovery call.

Is hosting included?

Hosting is separate but I'll help you set it up and recommend the right plan for your budget. Most clients spend $5–$20/month on hosting.

Can I cancel the monthly plan?

Yes — cancel anytime with no penalty. The monthly plan runs month-to-month with no long-term contract required.
